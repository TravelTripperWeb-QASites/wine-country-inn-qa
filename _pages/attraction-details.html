A few things to do in St Helena

There's no question that here in Napa Valley, it's all about the grapes. While Napa Valley has long established itself as a cultural attraction, the vast majority of people who come to St Helena and Napa Valley come for the wine. There are so many things to do in Napa Valley, from enjoying the local eateries to visiting St. Helena wineries. Wine tours are easily one of the more sought after things to do in Napa Valley, from the various wineries in the area to wine tastings to the Napa Wine Train.

Attractions

For those adventurous types, looking to explore

Whether it’s a new blend to try or an exhilarating hike through the valley’s picturesque hills, guests have much to explore throughout the Napa Valley.

Vineyards might be the main draw of wine country, but there are plenty of things to do - many of which just a few minutes from the Wine Country Inn & Cottages. From bike rides through the hills and hot air balloons over the Napa Valley to adventures through the numerous state parks in the area, guests have wine country at their fingertips.

Activities within 10 miles

Much of the Napa Valley’s best attractions are within minutes of the Wine Country Inn, thanks to its central location near the charming town of St. Helena.


  • Napa Valley Balloon Rides
  • The Culinary Institute of America
  • Bothe-Napa Valley State Park
  • St. Helena Farmer’s Market
  • Robert Louis Stevenson Museum
  • Calistoga Speedway
  • Bale Grist Mill Historic State Park

Activities within 30 miles

The Wine Country Inn & Cottages are also just a short drive from the towns of Calistoga and Yountville, as well as the valley’s namesake, Napa.


  • Napa Valley Wine Train
  • Mt. St. Helena Golf Course
  • Horseback Riding
  • Napa Valley Fairgrounds
  • Old Faithful Geyser of California
  • Petrified Forest
  • Uptown Theatre Napa
  • Napa Valley Bike Tours

Day Trips from the
Napa Valley

There’s also much to see outside the Napa Valley, including many of the country’s most impressive national parks, as well as California’s most intriguing cities, making a day trip from the Wine Country Inn well worth the drive.


  • Redwood National Park
  • San Francisco
  • Sacramento
  • Yosemite National Park
  • El Dorado National Forest

Groups & Events

A relaxing Napa Valley retreat for every occasion

Enjoy any special celebration or social event in California’s scenic Napa Valley wine country at the Wine Country Inn & Cottages. The pleasant and professional staff will ensure an unforgettable get-together or special occasion in an intimate wine country setting, while the hotel's Indoor and outdoor spaces are perfect for elopements, girls' weekends, family reunions, and special celebrations.

With 29 comfortable guestrooms and suites at this boutique bed and breakfast, the Wine Country Inn & Cottages is an ideal location for group events in an intimate setting. On site is The Living Room, a wonderful gathering place with comfortable seating and attractive furnishings, while hotel's the exterior grounds feature large patios, broad walkways, and outdoor spaces with beautiful vineyard views and mountain vistas, which provide a great backdrop for both weddings and get-togethers.

Girls' Trips

The Wine Country Inn & Cottages is not only great for getaways and vacations, but also for girls’ trips as well.

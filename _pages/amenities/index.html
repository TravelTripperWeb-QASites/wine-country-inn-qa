Unique details at a one-of-a-kind Napa Valley hotel

One of the most charming hotels in the Napa Valley, the Wine Country Inn & Cottages features comfortable accommodations and one-of-a-kind amenities that make the hotel a favorite among guests.

With vineyard views and private patios, baths and the highest-quality linens, the Wine Country Inn & Cottages mixes charming decor and comfortable amenities for a quintessential stay in the Napa Valley. Situated on the grounds of a local vineyard, the Wine Country Inn & Cottages is in the heart of wine country, close to the historic towns of St. Helena and Calistoga, as well as delicious restaurants, boutique shopping, and of course, world-class wineries.

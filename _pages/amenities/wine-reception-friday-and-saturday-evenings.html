Wine reception Friday & Saturday evenings

Spectacular Views and Luxurious Accommodations

Each Friday and Saturday night at the Wine Country Inn guests can enjoy a special wine tasting, which is hosted by one of Napa Valley’s finest wineries. Not only will winemakers pour their finest cabernets, chardonnays, and other varietals, but they will provide information on their winemaking process, insights on their favorite vintages, and maybe even some hidden Napa Valley wine secrets.

Our Luxury Amenities Include:

  • Wine Country Buffet Breakfast served each morning in the Winery House from 7:30-10:30 AM
  • Wine Reception Friday and Saturday evenings from 5:00pm-6:00 PM, hosted by a local vintner
  • Split of wine and assorted beverages in your guest room upon arrival
  • Heated swimming pool and Jacuzzi open from 8:00 AM-10:00 PM
  • In-room spa services available for purchase
  • Concierge services
  • Daily parking
  • No resort fee

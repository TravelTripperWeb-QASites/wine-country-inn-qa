Spectacular Views and Luxurious Accommodations

Wine Country Inn & Cottages is one of the most beautiful St. Helena hotels near Napa Valley. Our inn features luxury accommodations and amenities that you can’t find at other hotels or bed and breakfasts in the area. Imagine waking up each morning and being greeted by the picturesque views of the gorgeous Napa Valley vineyards in our select rooms and cottages. And, since we are situated right in St. Helena, our Napa Valley hotel is near the best wineries in the area. Whether you are traveling to the gorgeous Napa Valley to explore the breathtaking vineyards, delicious restaurants, or incredible landscape, the Wine Country Inn & Cottages in St. Helena, CA, is the ultimate retreat.

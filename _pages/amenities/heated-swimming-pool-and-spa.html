Heated swimming pool & spa

Relax at our Luxury Boutique Hotel Poolside in St. Helena

If you’re in search of a Napa Valley hotel with pools, our luxury hotel features a beautiful full-size rectangular pool with large areas for lounging in comfortable pool chaises and chairs. Our Napa Valley hotel pool in St. Helena provides the ultimate relaxation you are looking for after a long day of wine tasting in wine country. Enjoy a refreshing dip in the pool or the relaxing hot tub is an even better way to enjoy the beautiful expansive views that surround the outdoor Wine Country Inn & Cottages swimming pool oasis.

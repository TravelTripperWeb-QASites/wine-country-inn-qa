Reservations

We recommend that you book your Spa Treatment when you book your room.

Our Napa Valley spa treatments are offered from 9:00 am to 7:00 pm in your room.

Reservations can be made daily. To book an appointment at our Napa Valley spa hotel, please call the Wine Country Inn & Cottages Guest Service Manager.

When booking your treatment, please let us know if you prefer a male or female therapist or have any physical conditions that would limit your treatment.

24-Hour Cancellation Policy

If another diversion emerges, cancellations for treatments must be made at least 24 hours before the appointment or guests will be billed for the full price of the treatment you booked plus an 18% gratuity. To cancel please contact the Wine Country Inn & Cottages Guest Service Manager.

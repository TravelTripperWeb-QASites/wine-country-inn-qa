Complimentary wine country breakfast

A Gourmet Napa Valley Inn Breakfast

Every morning enjoy a sumptuous complimentary Wine Country breakfast in the Winery House of the Wine Country Inn & Cottages. Expect to be spoiled by delicious breakfast dishes in a bountiful buffet format. There is a daily assortment of fresh fruit, juices, eggs, cereals, breads, cheeses, and other local delicacies to nourish a busy day in Napa Valley wine country.

Breakfast is served daily at the Wine Country Inn & Cottages in the Winery House from 7:30 a.m. to 10:30 a.m. Enjoy a fresh home cooked meal upon waking to the beautiful vineyard views in Napa Valley. The buffet includes egg dishes, sliced meats and cheeses, hot and cold cereals, a variety of breads, and fresh fruits. Arrangements for gluten-free items are available for guests with food allergies.

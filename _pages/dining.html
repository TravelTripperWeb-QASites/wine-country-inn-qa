Dining

Experiences to please every palate

Food and wine are a quintessential part of the Napa Valley experience. From family wineries and comfort foods at roadside stands to acclaimed tasting menus eloquently paired with California wines, the Napa Valley knows how to wine and dine. And the staff at Wine Country Inn & Cottages is eager to make your experience special and can assist with reservations and recommendations.

Set in an actual vineyard, just five minutes north of the St. Helena town center, the Wine Country Inn & Cottages is among some of the Napa Valley’s best wineries and most renowned restaurants. From tastings at Freemark Abbey, Duckhorn Winery, Hall Winery, and Markham Winery to inventive cuisine Michelin-starred restaurants or stimulating cooking classes at The Culinary Institute of America, guests of the Wine Country Inn & Cottages have numerous vinicultural and gastronomic experiences at their fingertips.
